The Specialslaza App

Why build the app?

There is nothing else like it. This project's purpose was to create an application for students in the UC area to find restaurants and happy hour specials around campus. Google just lists local restaurants and bars who state they have happy hour, but they do not have everything listed in an easy-to-use format. This is a problem many college students and even those not in college face.

Technologies Used

As many as we could...

Google, Microsoft, and GitHub. We sought out to test and challenge ourselves with trying to include and use the best of modern technology that is available from the most well known companies. In the project we were able to leverage Google APIs, Microsoft Azure and some forms of Microsoft Power Platform, and a GitHub CI/CD pipeline.
